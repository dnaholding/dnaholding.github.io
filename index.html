About DnA holding

DnA Holding is a discerning family office dedicated to the meticulous management and strategic growth of intergenerational wealth. Beyond conventional portfolio management, our core ethos is to leverage capital as a catalyst for positive global change.

We are committed to nurturing ventures that push the boundaries of human knowledge and capability, fostering a legacy of innovation and betterment for all.

Our mission

To bridge the gap between financial stewardship and profound societal impact. DnA Holding invests with purpose, channeling resources into groundbreaking science and technology that directly addresses global challenges and enhances human well-being.

We seek to cultivate a future where prosperity is synonymous with progress, and where our investments yield both financial returns and transformative advancements for humanity.

Impact investments: advancing science & technology

Our venture capital arm specifically targets high-potential companies and research initiatives that are pioneering solutions in critical areas. We believe in the power of innovation to create a sustainable and thriving world.

Biotechnology & Healthcare

Investing in life sciences, novel therapeutics, diagnostics, and preventative health technologies to improve global health outcomes.

Sustainable Energy & Environment

Supporting renewable energy solutions, climate tech, sustainable agriculture, and technologies that protect our planet's ecosystems.

Artificial Intelligence & Robotics

Funding ethical AI development, advanced robotics, and automation that promise to enhance productivity and solve complex problems.

Advanced Materials & Nanotechnology

Exploring breakthroughs in materials science and nanotechnology that can revolutionize various industries, from manufacturing to medicine.

Space & Exploration

Backing ventures in space technology, satellite applications, and the exploration of new frontiers for scientific discovery and resource management.

Education & Human Capital tech

Empowering platforms and tools that democratize access to quality education and foster human potential globally.
